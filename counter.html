During the COVID-19 2020 Pandemic, our way of life changed in many ways. This lead to many creative ways for people to connect from a distance, something that would be essential during the months apart where little social interaction was had.

One of the ways we developed was the QR code.

While this small bit of technology was introduced in 1994, they reached a peak popularity in 2020 due to their accessability and ability to contribute to the distance rule put into effect during the pandemic.

These lead to intially unthought of goldmine for scammers. Due to how QR codes utilize hyperlinks, they act nearly exactly like regular links that you might click on in a word document. This can lead to scammer/hackers gaining access or sensitive personal information if the user is not careful. It makes it rather easy to trick the users, something profound during the Pandemic.

This website is our attempt to spread the awareness to you, dear internet dweller.
